Kisan bhaiyon, assalam walekum. Mein ne aap ki zameen aur mausam ko dekhte hue kuch mashware dene hain.

Pehle, aap juar (Sorghum) laga sakte hain. Ye taqreeban 120 din mein tayar ho jaye gi. Isko hafte mein aik bar pani dena hoga.

Dusra, gajar (Carrots) bhi achi fasal hai aap ke liye. Ye 75 din mein tayar ho jaye gi aur isko har do din baad pani dena chahiye.

Teesra, paalak (Spinach) bhi bohut jald tayar hone wali fasal hai. Ye sirf 45 din mein tayar ho jaye gi, lekin isko rozana pani dena hoga.

To bhai, ye teen faslein hain jo aap ke liye achi rahen gi. Apni marzi se faisla karein aur Allah barkat de.
